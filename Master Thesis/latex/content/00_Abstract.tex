\begin{abstract}

This thesis describes configuration-based mobile applications and their infrastructure. In addition, a possible way of implementing a configuration system is discussed.
Finally, the implementation is compared to the approach of established literature by classifying the model and interpreter.

Furthermore, there is an evaluation of the use case of interpreting and generated apps.



\end{abstract}
